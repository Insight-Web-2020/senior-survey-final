Personal

Indian Institute Of Technology Bombay, being the most esteemed engineering institute of India, has seen many facets of students when it comes to academics.

We surveyed the passing out batch to get a more thorough insight into students mentality towards their personal life here at the institute. We get to see many aspects and attitudes of students in general towards their personal life at IIT Bombay.

Here is an overview of the same.

  • A total of 215 students filled the survey form and presented their views across a wide range of questions ranging from Social Life to Relationships to Sexual Desires and many more.
  • 21.86% students spend less than 1 hour on social media, while 3.72% students spend whopping more than 6 hours.
  • The data shows that extrovertness of students has increased considerably during the stay at IIT Bombay.

Gender



What is your gender?

90.23% correspondents identified themselves as male while 8.3% as female. The rest preferred to choose the answers.

How did you identify yourself in freshman year / as now ?

96.71% students identified themselves as straight at the beginning of the freshie year while the number was 93.89% at the time of graduation. Only 1.87% were unsure of their sexual orientation as opposed to 2.35% at the beginning of the freshie year.



Social Life



How would you rate your social activity?

When asked to rate themselves on a scale of 5, w.r.t. their social activity, and most people seemed satisfied with it- as seen by the higher number of 4-5 ratings than the 1-2 ones.3.54 was the average rating people gave themselves on this meter.

How much time, on an average, did you spend on social media (i.e. Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Snapchat, Reddit etc.) in a day?

21.86% students spend less than 1 hour on social media, while 3.72% students spend whopping more than 6 hours.





Call yourself an extrovert or an introvert?

The data shows that extrovertness of students has increased considerably during the stay at IIT Bombay.

Demographics

You primarily grew up in a?

32.56% students grew up in big cities, 5.12% students grew up in villages and rural areas. Rest of the students grew up in small cities and towns





Which region would you call your native place?

A vast majority of students identified Maharashtra as their home. Rajasthan came second closely followed by Madhya Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh and Gujarat.

Family & Relationships

How close were you to your family while at college?

60.93% students stated that they were very close to their family and they talked regularly.

Whereas 2.79% students said that they didn't maintain a very good relations with family.





How many times you were in relationship during your stay?

We sought to know the number of relationships each person had been in, and got to know not-so-surprising results, with almost half people having no relationships, and almost all others in 1-2 relationships. Very few (only 6.5%) of our responders have had more than 2 relationships.

Have you dated someone while at IIT?

55.29% students replied that they dated someone from or outside the Insti while 44.23% said that they never dated anyone.

0.48% students said that they tried to date someone but were unsuccessfull.





How was your interaction with opposite sex?

A sizeable amount of people (2.3%) knew no one from the opposite sex, even as acquaintances, while another group (of about 6% people) had more close friends in the opposite sex.

A large number of people had few acquaintances in the opposite sex, while an equal number had a few close friends. A few (13.5%) people had many acquaintances in the opposite sex

How would you prefer to live after leaving the institute?

46.98% students stated that they will prefer to live with their friends after leaving institute, while 8.84% students will prefer to live with their parents.





When do you plan to get married?

0.47% students are already married while most of the students are planning to get married in future.

40% students don't have any idea about their marriage. 8.38% students don't want to get married at all!



Which dating app did you use?

When asked about the use of dating apps, maximum people said that they never used one. Most others had used dating apps rarely, and very few people had used them often.

Sexual Desires

How often did you have sex while at IIT Bombay?

Given the sex ratio of the institute (and well, engineering in general); and the relationship stats, this was a rather not-so-unexpected result; with about two-thirds of people having never had sex in insti life.





How often did you watch porn?

When asked about their porn habits, most students confessed that they have watched it regularly, at least for a part of their stay. However, around 10.7% students said that they have watched porn rarely, while a sizeable (6%) portion of students said that they had never watched porn. About 3.7% students preferred to skip the question.
