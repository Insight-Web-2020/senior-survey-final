Career

Indian Institute Of Technology Bombay, being the most esteemed engineering institute of India, has seen many facets of students when it comes to academics.

We surveyed the passing out batch to get a more thorough insight into students mentality towards their career. We get to see many aspects and attitudes of students in general towards their career choices after IIT Bombay.

Here is an overview of the same.

  • 74.29% of the students will be staying in India only. Some students are also going to the USA.
  • More than half of the correspondents, 54.98% , reacted in a negative manner of changing mind regarding sector after completing a major intern, and 45.02% reacted in a affirmative manner.
  • GRE is the most popular choice among the students planning for further studies. GATE being the least popular one.

Career Path

What are you doing right now/will be doing a few months from now?

38.1% correspondents are working in Technological Sector while 20.95% are pursuing higher studies. Other majority is 12.38% students working in the corporate non-core sector.

3.33% said that they had no clue.



Are you interested in the career line you have choosen?

Almost 53% of the people are happy with whatever career path they have decided to follow, whereas almost 40% of the people are also not very sure of what they want to do and are still exploring.



In which field are you going to work?

Some students have planned to pursue further studies and research in their field of interest. It is not very surprising to see that some of them don’t have a clue about what they’ll be doing 5 years down the line.



Have you taken/plan on taking any higher studies exam?

GRE is the most popular choice among the students planning for further studies. GATE being the least popular one.



Posting

Where have been you posted?

74.29% of the students will be staying in India only. Some students are also going to the USA.



Where are you posted in India?

Most of the graduating students will be heading off to Bangalore, followed by Mumbai.



Where do you wish you were heading off to?

It’s not shocking to notice that most of the students wanted to stay in India only, and that’s what they will be doing as suggested by the previous graph.



Why do you want to go abroad?

129 students want to go abroad beacause they think the lifestyle and opportunities are better there, as compared to India.

63 students don't want to go abroad.



Core Interest

Would you have pursued the core sector had there been more opportunities?

Majority of the correspondents ,40.76% , said ‘question not applicable to me’ and 32.23% correspondents reacted in a affirmative manner and rest in a negative manner.



Internships

Have you had a change of mind regarding the sector of your career after completing a major intern?

More than half of the correspondents, 54.98% , reacted in a negative manner of changing mind regarding sector after completing a major intern, and 45.02% reacted in a affirmative manner.



How much did your internships help you in making an effective career choice?

According to the above plot, we can say that most of the students have either ‘massive influence’ or ‘mediocre influence’ and few have ‘made no difference’ and some of them are ‘are not sure’ , but there are very few who didn’t intern anywhere.
