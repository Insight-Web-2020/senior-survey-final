Lifestyle

Indian Institute Of Technology Bombay, being the most esteemed engineering institute of India, has seen many facets of students when it comes to academics.

We surveyed the passing out batch to get a more thorough insight into students mentality towards their lifestyle here at the institute. We get to see many aspects and attitudes of students in general towards their lifestyle at IIT Bombay.

Here is an overview of the same.

  • A total of 215 students filled the survey form and presented their views across a wide range of questions ranging from General Lifestyle to Mental Health to Smoking Habits and many more.
  • Around 36.28% of the students pegged their monthly expenditure between 1000 and 3000 Rupees, while around 29% of the respondents spend over 5000 Rupees per month.
  • Most of the students did not have any smoking habits, while a considerable (8.4%) number of our responders admitted to being chain smokers, who smoked around a pack per day. Around 1-10th students admitted to smoking rarely(once in two-three weeks).

General Lifestyle

How would you rate yourself on a scale of 5, regarding how disciplined and regular you were in the aspects of Sleep, Food, hygiene and Health?

We found that people, as a group, were concerned about Hygiene the most, followed by Food, and health. They were least concerned about their sleep schedule.



Which of these places have you visited during your stay at IIT- Bombay?

Library is the place where students had visited most frequently followed by Boat House and Sameer Hill.



Mental Health

How frequently did you consult a psychiatrist/doctor for mental health issues?

More than half of our responders never needed it. Around a fifth needed help, but chose to not go for consult with a doctor. Around a fourth have actually taken help from Professionals.



Hygiene

How often did you take a bath? (on an average)

38.48% students used to take a bath everyday. More than 50% students had a bath once in two or three days.

0.47% students a bath once a week/twice a semester.



Smoking Habits

How often did you smoke?

Most of the students did not have any smoking habits, while a considerable (8.4%) number of our responders admitted to being chain smokers, who smoked around a pack per day. Around 1-10th students admitted to smoking rarely(once in two-three weeks).



Expenditure

Where do you peg your spending per month(in Rupees)?

Around 36.28% of the students pegged their monthly expenditure between 1000 and 3000 Rupees, while around 29% of the respondents spend over 5000 Rupees per month.



What was the major cause of your spending?

Highest 85.12% spendings were on Food, followed by 6.51% on Travel, 6.05% on Alcohol and 2.33% on others.
