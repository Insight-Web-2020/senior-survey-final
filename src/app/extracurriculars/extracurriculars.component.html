Extracurricular Activities

Indian Institute Of Technology Bombay, being the most esteemed engineering institute of India, has seen many facets of students when it comes to academics.

We surveyed the passing out batch to get a more thorough insight into students mentality towards the extracurriculars they have done here at the institute. We get to see many aspects and attitudes of students in general towards them.

Here is an overview of the same.

  • Among the total responses, 3.273 responses out of 5 are affirmative that Position of Responsibility has benefited them.
  • Majority of the correspondents responded that they developed their Organizational skills ,followed by Technical ,Sport ,Cultural and others. Very few responded as they did not pick any skill.
  • Majority of the students are either politically unbiased or inclined towards left.
